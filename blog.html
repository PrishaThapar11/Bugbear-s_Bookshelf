Code, Coffee, and the Constant Quest for Semicolons

Jan 15, 2025 | By Prisha Thapar | Type: Tech

I'm always intrigued by how learning to code feels like two things at once - on a good day it feels like stepping into a new world, where every line of code is an adventure and a chance to create something of value. On an even better day (initially), it's like trying to put together a puzzle with half the pieces missing- and no picture on the box. I've had more than my fair share of “why won't this work?” moments, I've stared at my screen, convinced that my code is cursed, only to realize I missed a semicolon. I've spent hours debugging, only to find that the issue was the tiniest, most ridiculous mistake. but each time I figure out the problem I learn something new. I'm not just writing code, I'm learning to problem-solve, think critically, work hard and get hands-on. This journey is as rewarding as it is challenging. .
